About Me

My name is Leonardo Ramos, and I have been coding since I was 13 years old. I started with C# and Unity, but now I am more focused on other languages. I am passionate about game development and programming in general. I love to create new projects and learn new technologies.

I am currently working on a project called Armory App thats an app that helps you manage your game library. It is built with React and Node.js, and it is available on GitHub. I am also working on a game called Project: ZombieGame that is a survival game where you have to survive against hordes of zombies. It is built with Unity and C#. I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to learn and grow as a developer.
